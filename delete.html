UIRS — Account & Data Deletion

How to request permanent removal of your account and personal data from the Unified Incident Response System (UIRS). This page describes the scope, verification steps, what will be deleted, what may be retained, timelines, and next steps.

Privacy & Data Rights

Please Contact rogu.erdao.ui@phinmaed.com for assistance with your data deletion request.

How to Submit a Deletion Request

UIRS is committed to respecting users’ rights to control their personal data. If you wish to have your account and associated personal data removed, submit a deletion request to our Data Protection team. The following steps outline the submission, verification, and execution process to ensure requests are handled securely and in compliance with applicable law.

Step-by-step submission

  1. Prepare your request: Include your full legal name, the email address used to register with UIRS, your phone number (if registered), and a short statement that you are requesting account deletion.
  2. Send the request: Email the request to rogu.erdao.ui@phinmaed.com. Use the subject line: UIRS Account Deletion Request — [Your Full Name].
  3. Identity verification: For security, we will verify your identity using one or more of the methods described below.
  4. Confirmation & processing: After verification, you will receive a confirmation and an estimated completion date. The deletion will proceed as described in this policy.

Identity verification

To protect privacy and prevent unauthorized deletions, UIRS will verify the account owner prior to processing a deletion. Verification may include:

  • Email verification (confirmation sent to the registered address)
  • Two-factor challenge if enabled on the account
  • Submission of a small number of recent account activity details (e.g., date of last login, recent report ID) to confirm ownership

Eligibility

Account owners and authorized representatives may request deletion. We may require additional documentation for legal representatives acting on behalf of another individual.

What will be deleted

Subject to verification and any applicable legal obligations, UIRS will remove personally identifiable data linked to your account, including but not limited to:

  • Account profile information (name, photo, username)
  • Contact information (email addresses, phone numbers)
  • Uploaded media (images) that contain personal identifiers
  • Personal attachments and notes
  • Incident reports that directly identify you; where an incident is shared or co-authored, personal identifiers will be removed or redacted

What may be retained and why

In some situations, UIRS will retain limited or anonymized records for legitimate business, legal, or safety purposes. Common reasons include:

  • Legal obligations such as law enforcement requests or preservation orders
  • Retention of anonymized statistics, aggregated analytics, and operational logs that do not identify you
  • Records required for fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or to protect the rights and safety of others
Note: UIRS will not attempt to re-identify anonymized data after deletion. Aggregated statistics used for system improvements will not be linked to personal identifiers.

Processing time & timeline

  • Receipt & acknowledgment (within 3 business days): We confirm receipt and outline verification steps.
  • Verification (variable, typically 3–10 business days): Identity checks to ensure only authorized requests are processed.
  • Deletion processing (up to 30 days after verification): Execution of deletion steps across primary systems and backups as feasible.
  • Final confirmation: You will receive an email confirming completion and explaining what was retained (if anything) and why.

Legal exceptions and holds

UIRS may decline or partially fulfill deletion requests when there are legal obligations to retain data (e.g., court orders, regulatory requirements), or when the data is necessary for ongoing investigations or to protect public safety. In these cases we will:

  • Clearly explain the legal basis for retention
  • Limit access and apply security controls while the data is retained
  • Delete or anonymize the data as soon as the retention obligation expires

Appeals and corrections

If you disagree with the result of a deletion request, contact the Data Protection team at rogu.erdao.ui@phinmaed.com with the subject "Appeal — Account Deletion". Provide the confirmation reference included in the deletion notice. We will review appeals promptly and provide a written response explaining the outcome.

FAQs

Can I get a copy of my data before deletion?
Yes — request a data export prior to deletion. Exports will be provided in a standard format and must be requested separately.

Will deletion affect others?
Shared content may be redacted or disassociated from your identity, but content that includes other users may remain in anonymized or redacted form.

Will backups be immediately deleted?
Backups are typically retained for operational reasons; personal identifiers will be removed from primary systems, and backups will be purged in accordance with our backup retention policy (generally within 30–90 days).

Security & Accountability

All deletion requests are logged and audited. Only authorized privacy team members may approve or carry out deletions. We maintain records of processed requests to support accountability and to comply with applicable legal obligations.
