UIRS Account and Data Deletion

This page explains the procedure for requesting the removal of an account and the personal data linked to it in the Unified Incident Response System (UIRS).

How to Submit a Deletion Request

Send an email to rogu.erdao.ui@phinmaed.com with the subject line: Account Deletion Request.

Include the following details:

• Full name
• Registered email address
• Registered phone number

Requests are verified to confirm identity before processing.

Data Removed After Approval

The following data is erased after your request is approved:

• Account profile
• Contact information
• Uploaded images
• Uploaded audio
• Incident reports linked to your identity

Data Retained

The system retains anonymized incident records for statistical and operational purposes. These records hold no link to your identity.

Processing Time

Personal data removal is completed within 30 days after request approval.

Support

For questions related to account removal or data retention, contact rogu.erdao.ui@phinmaed.com.
