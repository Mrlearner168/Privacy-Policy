UIRS App — Privacy Policy

A clear explanation of what we collect, why we collect it, how we protect it, and the rights available to you as a user of the Unified Incident Response System.

Effective: November 26, 2025
Contact privacy team

Introduction

The Unified Incident Response System (UIRS) is designed to facilitate timely reporting and coordination of incidents that affect public safety and operations. Protecting your personal information is a core responsibility for UIRS. This policy describes what information we collect, how we use and share it, retention practices, safeguards we apply, and the choices available to you.

Scope

This policy applies to individuals who use the UIRS mobile app and related services. It covers registered users, anonymous reporters, and any third parties who submit information through UIRS integrations.

Information we collect

We collect only the information necessary to deliver incident response capabilities and maintain the platform:

  • Account details: name, email address, phone number, profile photo (optional), and account settings.
  • Incident data: incident descriptions, timestamps, geolocation data provided at report time, attachments (photos, audio, video), and related metadata.
  • Device & diagnostics: device type, OS version, app version, telemetry and error logs to help diagnose issues and improve reliability.
  • Usage data: interaction logs and anonymized analytics to improve features and measure service performance.

How we use collected information

  • To process incident reports and coordinate responses with authorized responders.
  • To communicate with users about incident status, account matters, and critical notifications.
  • To secure and maintain the service, investigate abuse, and prevent fraud.
  • To perform aggregated analytics that do not identify individuals for service improvement.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. Sharing is limited to the following:

  • Authorized emergency or response agencies when necessary to address an incident.
  • Service providers who process data on our behalf under contract (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics).
  • When required by law or to protect rights, safety, or property (e.g., lawful requests from authorities).
Important: When incident reports include other persons' information, UIRS treats that content with care and applies redaction or access controls where appropriate.

Data retention & deletes

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the service and to comply with legal obligations. Anonymized records and aggregated analytics may be retained indefinitely for system improvement. Account deletion requests are honored subject to verification. Backups may persist for a limited period; personal identifiers are removed from primary systems and purged from backups in accordance with our retention schedule.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights including access, rectification, deletion, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection. To exercise these rights contact us at rogu.erdao.ui@phinmaed.com.

Security measures

We employ administrative, technical, and physical safeguards — including encryption in transit, role-based access control, logging, and regular security assessments — to protect data. However, no system is impervious; users should secure their credentials and enable two-factor authentication where available.

International transfers

Data may be processed in jurisdictions outside your country. When transfers occur, we enforce contractual safeguards and applicable legal mechanisms to protect your information.

Children

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such data, we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Notice of material changes will be provided by posting an updated policy and, where appropriate, notifying registered users.
